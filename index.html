Magnets

In the youtube video I use 4 these 65 pound pull strength magnets to very securely attach one Azeron. However they are not currently available, so consider these alternatives:


40 lb pull strength

Compact enough that you could fit 6 inside an Altoids tin.


75 lb pull strength

Fits 3 or 4 in a tin.

The hook can be conveniently screwed off after placing in the tin

RAM X-Grip Universal Phone Holder with Ball
B-Size RAM double socket arm

Available in different lengths

The background picture shows one short and one medium

You may want to experiment with whatever works for your forearm length, mounting point, and ergonomic preferences


I recommend sticking to ball size "B", because the X-Grip phone holder only accomidates that size.

RAM Tough-Claw Medium Clamp Base with Ball "B"

Avoid off-brand substitutes that have a low-friction ball. They will fail to hold up the Azeron.


RAM Tough-Claw Small Clamp Base with Ball "B"

This smaller clamp will grip smaller objects


As previously stated, the X-Grip phone holder only accomidates ball size "B". Fortunately, the balls on these RAM mounts are high friction, so they can hold up the Azeron.


Monitor Mount with laptop tray

For positioning laptop at eye level. It is highly adjustable and can be swiveled to the side when not in use.

Monitor Mount

Very similar to above, but this brand does not come with a tray module. It serves as an adjustable arm suitable for suspending the Azerons over your body.
